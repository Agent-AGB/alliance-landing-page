How do Brockton winters affect my roof and when should I replace it?

Brockton winters bring heavy snow loads, ice dams, and freeze-thaw cycles that accelerate roof deterioration. If your roof is more than 20 years old, shows signs of granule loss, curling shingles, or interior leaks after winter storms, it is time for a professional assessment. Fall is the ideal season to schedule a replacement before harsh conditions arrive.
