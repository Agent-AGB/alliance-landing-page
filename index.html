Yes. Alliance Group Builders LLC is fully licensed and insured in the state of Massachusetts. We carry full liability insurance and workers compensation on every project.

Yes! We offer flexible payment options so you can get the roof, windows, addition, or remodel you need NOW without waiting or draining your savings. Qualified homeowners can get started with manageable monthly payments. Ask us for details when you request your free estimate.
How fast can you provide an estimate? ▼
We respond to all estimate requests within 24 hours. In most cases we can schedule a free in-home consultation within the same week depending on availability.
What areas do you serve? ▼
We serve Eastern Massachusetts from Worcester and beyond — including the South Shore, Boston Metro, MetroWest, the North Shore, Cape Cod, Martha's Vineyard, and Nantucket.
Do you handle permits? ▼
Yes. We handle all required permits for your project so you do not have to worry about the paperwork. Everything is done by the book, protecting you and your property.
Do you offer warranties on your work? ▼
Yes. We stand behind our work with a warranty on all projects. Ask us about specific warranty terms for your project type when you request your estimate.
